❤️ Health Tips

  • Brush your pet’s teeth at least once a week.
  • Always use pet-safe toothpaste, never human toothpaste.
  • Dental chews help reduce plaque and tartar.
  • Check gums regularly for redness or swelling.
  • Bad breath can be a sign of dental disease.
  • Schedule annual dental checkups with a vet.
  • Provide chew toys to strengthen jaw muscles.
  • Avoid feeding too many sugary treats.
  • Dental sprays can help maintain oral hygiene.
  • Early dental care prevents tooth loss and infections.

  • Provide a balanced diet suitable for your pet’s age.
  • Always keep fresh and clean water available.
  • Avoid feeding human food to pets.
  • Follow proper portion sizes to prevent obesity.
  • High-quality protein is essential for muscle health.
  • Vitamins and minerals support immunity.
  • Avoid sudden food changes to prevent stomach upset.
  • Senior pets may require special diets.
  • Do not overfeed treats and snacks.
  • Consult a vet before changing diet plans.
